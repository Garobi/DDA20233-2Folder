Pilar

Pilar was an edgerunner of Night City, and was described as a comical and crude techie. He and his sister Rebecca often team up with Maine and his crew to conduct mercenary jobs.

Pilar met his demise after jesting and taunting a cyberpsycho, who shot him in the head with their Projectile Launch System from close range, destroying most of Pilar's skull.
