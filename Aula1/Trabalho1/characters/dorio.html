Dorio

Dorio is a veteran Edgerunner and Maine's right-hand, as well as a member of Maine's Edgerunner crew. She's jacked and strong, but despite her tough exterior, Dorio has a soft side that she only shows to the people she trusts

Dorio is shown to be caring and nice to those she trusts, predominantly to Maine.
