Ripperdoc

A surgeon by day, Doc moonlights as an illicit ripperdoc. An active businessman, Doc also operates sidehustles selling braindances, software, drugs and other items often found on the black market.

Shameless and hedonistic, Doc's penchant for opportunism and ruthless negotiation have a notable effect on the quality of life of his business associate, David Martinez.
